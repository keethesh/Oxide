# Code of Conduct

Oxide is a technical project, but the standard for participation is still simple: be respectful, stay constructive, and keep disagreement focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss code, behavior, and evidence directly
- assume good intent unless there is clear evidence otherwise
- accept correction without escalating into personal conflict
- keep critique specific and actionable
- respect the time and attention of other contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostility
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- spam, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith reporting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project spaces.
